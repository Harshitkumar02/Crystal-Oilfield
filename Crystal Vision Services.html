Structural Steel Fabrication

High-capacity fabrication up to 1200 tons per month for large-scale industrial and infrastructure projects.

Crystal Vision Steel Fabrication provides high-capacity structural steel fabrication services designed to support large-scale industrial, oil & gas, power, infrastructure, and offshore projects with strict dimensional accuracy and weld integrity.

Key Capabilities:

  • High-volume fabrication capability for fast-track projects
  • Precision fabrication using CNC cutting and advanced welding
  • Strict dimensional control and quality inspection
  • Compliance with ASTM, ASME, AISC, and ISO standards

Light, Medium & Heavy Steel Structures

Complete fabrication across all structural complexity levels from light supports to heavy industrial frameworks.

Specialized fabrication of beams, columns, trusses, frames, pipe racks, platforms, staircases, ladders, handrails, and equipment support structures engineered to withstand operational loads and environmental conditions.

Key Capabilities:

  • Load-bearing and non-load-bearing structures
  • Custom-designed structures based on client drawings
  • Suitable for onshore plants, offshore modules, warehouses
  • High structural integrity with certified welding procedures

Piping Fabrication

High-capacity piping spool fabrication up to 12,000 Dia-Inch per month for process plants and refineries.

Comprehensive piping spool fabrication services including cutting, beveling, fit-up, welding, NDT, hydrotesting, surface treatment for both carbon and alloy piping systems.

Key Capabilities:

  • High-capacity spool fabrication for fast project execution
  • Compliance with ASME B31.3, B31.4, and B31.8 standards
  • In-house testing and inspection facilities
  • Suitable for oil, gas, petrochemical, and utility pipelines

Material Handling & Fabrication

Expert handling of CS, SS, SDSS, and LTCS materials with complete traceability and quality control.

Proven capability in handling Carbon Steel (CS), Stainless Steel (SS), Super Duplex Stainless Steel (SDSS), and Low-Temperature Carbon Steel (LTCS) with material traceability, PMI testing, and controlled welding.

Key Capabilities:

  • Corrosion-resistant and low-temperature materials
  • Strict material traceability and quality documentation
  • Suitable for sour service, cryogenic, and offshore environments
  • Project-specific metallurgical requirements compliance

  • Quality inspection and dimensional verification

  • Extended service life of steel structures
  • Suitable for offshore and aggressive environments
