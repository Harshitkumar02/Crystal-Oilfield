COMPREHENSIVE SERVICES

Oil & Gas Engineering Excellence

From civil construction to specialized oil & gas project works, delivering complete turnkey solutions with uncompromising quality and safety standards

Civil Construction

Comprehensive civil construction services for industrial, oil & gas, power, and infrastructure projects from site preparation to final finishing.

Civil works executed to support heavy industrial loads, operational safety, and long-term durability in harsh environmental conditions typical of oil & gas facilities.

Key Capabilities:

  • Earthworks, excavation, backfilling, and compaction
  • Foundations for equipment, tanks, pipe racks, and structures
  • Concrete works (RCC, PCC, precast where applicable)
  • Drainage systems, bund walls, and underground utilities
  • Compliance with QCS, ASTM, and international standards

Oil & Gas Project Works

End-to-end oil & gas project execution covering onshore facilities, refineries, terminals, and gas processing plants.

Crystal Oilfield leverages EPC experience and multidisciplinary teams to manage complex interfaces between civil, mechanical, piping, E&I, and safety systems.

Key Capabilities:

  • Onshore oil & gas facilities execution
  • Coordination of multi-discipline construction activities
  • Compliance with client and regulatory requirements
  • Focus on schedule control, safety, and quality

Hard & Soft Landscaping Works

Integrated hard and soft landscaping solutions for industrial and infrastructure projects meeting functional and environmental requirements.

Landscaping designed to meet functional, environmental, and aesthetic requirements while complying with local environmental guidelines.

Key Capabilities:

  • Hard landscaping: pavements, kerbs, walkways, retaining walls
  • Soft landscaping: soil preparation, planting, turfing, irrigation
  • Landscaping for industrial, commercial, and infrastructure sites
  • Sustainable and low-maintenance landscape solutions

Tank Farm Construction

Specialized tank farm construction for petroleum and chemical products storage with complete civil, mechanical, and structural works.

Projects executed in line with international codes such as API 650, API 653, and NFPA, ensuring safety, environmental protection, and operational reliability.

Key Capabilities:

  • Construction of tank foundations and bund walls
  • Installation of storage tanks and associated piping
  • Firefighting, drainage, and safety systems
  • Compliance with petroleum storage standards

Oil & Gas Plant Erection

Complete plant erection services including mechanical equipment, structural steel, and piping systems installation.

Erection activities carefully planned to minimize downtime and ensure safe execution with coordination with commissioning teams.

Key Capabilities:

  • Installation of static and rotating equipment
  • Structural steel and pipe rack erection
  • Alignment, leveling, and grouting works
  • Coordination with commissioning teams

Hydrotesting of Pipelines

Hydrostatic testing services for pipelines and piping systems to verify strength, integrity, and leak-tightness.

Testing carried out using calibrated equipment and approved procedures with complete documentation and certification.

Key Capabilities:

  • Hydrotesting of onshore pipelines and piping networks
  • Test pressure as per ASME and project specifications
  • Documentation and test certification
  • Safe disposal and environmental compliance

Asset Integrity & Reliability

Asset integrity and reliability services maintaining safe, efficient, and compliant operations throughout the asset lifecycle.

Services focus on risk mitigation, inspection, and preventive maintenance with compliance to regulatory and safety requirements.

Key Capabilities:

  • Inspection and condition assessment of assets
  • Corrosion monitoring and integrity evaluation
  • Reliability improvement strategies
  • Compliance with regulatory and safety requirements

Plant Change & Modification

Brownfield and modification projects including plant upgrades, system rerouting, and capacity enhancement.

Works performed while minimizing operational disruption with strict safety control and coordination with ongoing plant operations.

Key Capabilities:

  • Tie-ins, rerouting, and system upgrades
  • Equipment replacement and retrofitting
  • Brownfield execution with strict safety control
  • Coordination with ongoing plant operations

Infrastructure Development

Infrastructure development projects supporting industrial zones, terminals, and utilities with long-term durability.

These works provide the backbone required for large-scale industrial operations with compliance to international standards.

Key Capabilities:

  • Roads, drainage networks, and utility corridors
  • Underground services and duct banks
  • Industrial infrastructure for oil & gas facilities
  • Long-term durability and compliance with standards

Petroleum Storage Tank Design & Fabrication

Complete tank solutions covering engineering, fabrication, erection, and testing of petroleum storage tanks.

Projects executed under stringent quality and safety controls with compliance to API standards for reliable storage solutions.

Key Capabilities:

  • Engineering and design as per API standards
  • Fabrication and erection of steel storage tanks
  • Hydrotesting and commissioning support
  • Suitable for crude oil, fuel, and chemical storage

MEP & HVAC Project Works

Complete MEP and HVAC services for industrial, commercial, and infrastructure facilities ensuring operational efficiency.

Systems designed and installed to ensure operational efficiency, safety, and compliance with international energy standards.

Key Capabilities:

  • Mechanical, electrical, and plumbing installations
  • HVAC systems for industrial and commercial buildings
  • Testing, balancing, and commissioning
  • Compliance with international MEP standards

Water Jet Cutting for Oil & Gas

High-pressure water jet cutting services for oil & gas applications enabling safe cold cutting without heat-affected zones.

Ideal for hazardous environments where hot work is restricted, providing precision cutting without thermal distortion and reduced risk.

Key Capabilities:

  • Cold cutting for pipes, tanks, and structures
  • Safe execution in live plants and hazardous zones
  • Precision cutting without thermal distortion
  • Reduced risk compared to conventional cutting
