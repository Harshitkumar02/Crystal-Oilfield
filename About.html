Introduction to Crystal Oilfield

Crystal Oilfield Contracting and Trading W.L.L, a distinguished member of the Al Bahr Al Arabi Group (UAE), was established in Qatar in 2024.

We provide specialized EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) and maintenance services for the Oil & Gas, Power, and Desalination industries.

OUR DIRECTION

Vision, Mission & Values

Guiding principles that drive our commitment to excellence and sustainable growth in the oil and gas industry.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading provider of comprehensive construction and engineering solutions in the MENA region, specializing in Oil & Gas projects, MEP contracting, fabrication of piping spools, structural works, skids, steel welded tanks, and advanced engineering services. We aim to be the preferred partner for our clients by consistently exceeding expectations through our unwavering commitment to excellence, professionalism, and integrity.

Our Mission

We are committed to delivering exceptional Oil & Gas services through innovative, technology-driven solutions. By upholding the highest standards of quality, safety, and environmental sustainability, we aim to drive growth and development for our clients and communities.

Our Core Values

The fundamental principles that guide our operations and define our corporate culture.

Quality

We are dedicated to delivering superior quality in every aspect of our work, ensuring that our services meet or exceed industry standards.

Safety

The safety of our employees, clients, and stakeholders is our top priority. We are committed to maintaining a safe and healthy work environment.

Integrity

We conduct business with honesty, transparency, and ethical practices. We value trust and strive to build long-lasting relationships with our clients and partners.

Innovation

We embrace innovation and continuously seek new technologies and approaches to improve our services and meet the evolving needs of our clients.

Teamwork

We foster a collaborative work culture that promotes teamwork, mutual respect, and open communication. We believe in the power of collective effort to achieve success.

Customer Focus

Our clients are at the center of everything we do. We listen to their needs, provide tailored solutions, and strive to exceed their expectations.

Sustainability

We are committed to minimizing our environmental impact and promoting sustainable practices in our operations. We aim to contribute to a greener and more sustainable future.

OUR LEADERSHIP

Executive Leadership

Meet the visionary leaders driving our success and strategic growth across global markets.

Muhammed Akram

Director

Al Bahr Al Arabi Group Owner

Mr. Muhammed Akram, a distinguished Board Member and owner of Al Bahr Al Arabi Group (est. 1998, UAE), brings extensive expertise in the Oil & Gas sector, particularly in petroleum storage tank construction. He also owns a fully operational oil storage terminal in Al Hamriyah Free Zone.

Key Achievement

Under his leadership, Al Bahr has successfully completed 65 oil storage terminals in Hamriyah Free Zone, contributing significantly to the region's energy infrastructure and the company's continued growth.

26+ Years ExperienceGroup Founder

Mohammed Kazim Khan

General Manager

Operations Leader

Mr. Mohammed Kazim Khan is an accomplished leader with over 25 years of experience in the Oil & Gas sector across India and Qatar. As General Manager, he oversees the company's Oil & Gas operations, including steel fabrication and project management, ensuring excellence in quality, safety, and timely delivery.

Major Projects

He has successfully led major EPIC projects for QatarEnergy, including the CNG Bus Filling Stations at MIC & RLIC and DPFU Phase-1 Mechanical Completion at Dukhan. His extensive experience in Ras Laffan, Dukhan, and Mesaieed strengthens the company's operational capabilities.
